!1 Logon User:
Users can log on by providing their username and password.
If both match, the users is logged in.

!1 Examples:
In the examples below 2 failing login attemps are made.
Once with an invalid password and once with an invalid username.
Finally providing a valid username and password is successful.

!|ActionFixture|
|Start|Login script|
|Enter|Username|michel|
|Enter|Password|blah|
|Press|Login|
|Check|Logged in|false|
|Enter|Username|mike|
|Enter|Password|iLean|
|Press|Login|
|Check|Logged in|false|
|Enter|Username|michel|
|Enter|Password|ilean|
|Press|Login|
|Check|Logged in|true|
