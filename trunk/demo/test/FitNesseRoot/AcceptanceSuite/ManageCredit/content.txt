!1 Manage Credit:
Credits can be added and deducted for a user.
After creating a user, the credit is 0.

!3 Given
User 'pascal' is created with no credit.
!|Insert|
|Add User|pascal|with password|ihc|

!3 When - Then
When 10 credits are added to his account, he has 1O credits.
!|Manage users credit|
|Check|Credits for user|pascal|is|0|
|Add|10|Credits for|pascal||
|Check|Credits for user|pascal|is|10|

When 5 credits are deducted from his account, he has 5 credits left.
!|Manage users credit|
|Deduct|5|Credits for|pascal|
|Check|Credits for user|pascal|is|5|
