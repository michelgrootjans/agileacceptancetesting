!1 Order Snack For User With Insufficient Credit:
Users can't place orders that would exceed their credit.

!3 Given:
Joe is an existing user with €5 credit.
Pizza Hawaii is a valid snack of €5,3 euro.

!|Insert|
|Add Snack|Pizza Hawaii|with price|5,3|
|Add User|Joe|with credit|5|

!3 When - then:
When Joe tries to order a Pizza Hawaii, he gets an error message.

!|Order Snack|
|Qty|Snack|For user|Result?|
|1|Pizza Hawaii|Joe|You don't have enough credit. You need €0,3 extra.|
